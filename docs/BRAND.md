# Brand — TechEze AI / Best Computer Tech LLC

## Brand Promise
Fast launch. Clear pricing. Built to convert.

## Positioning
Not a “website designer.” A “lead generation website builder” for local businesses.

## Voice & Tone
- Direct, confident, results-focused
- Minimal fluff
- Simple language that business owners understand
- Emphasize speed + outcomes + professionalism

## Key Phrases
- “Built to generate calls & leads”
- “Fixed price. Fast launch.”
- “No agency timelines. No template junk.”
- “Conversion-first design”
- “Your site goes live—fast.”

## Trust Signals to Include
- Clear process + timeline
- Realistic deliverables & boundaries
- Guarantee framing (on-time launch)
- Maintenance plan options
- Security/modern stack credibility

Primary: TechEze AI
