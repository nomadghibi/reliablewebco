# Intake Form Spec

## For 24-Hour Landing
Fields:
- Business name
- Niche (HVAC, cleaning, etc.)
- City/Service area
- Phone + email
- Primary offer (what are they selling?)
- Top 3 services
- What makes you different?
- Social proof (reviews link, before/after, photos)
- Desired CTA (call, text, book, form)
- Brand assets upload (logo, images)
