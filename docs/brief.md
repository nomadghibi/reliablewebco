# WebsiteSprint — Brief

## Goal
Sell fast, fixed-scope website builds to generate quick cashflow:
- 24-hour landing page = $499
- Full “Website-in-a-Week” = $2,000+
Add monthly care plans for recurring revenue.

## Target customers
Local service businesses + solo pros:
HVAC, cleaning, dental, landscaping, home care, contractors, lawyers, etc.

## Positioning
“High-converting websites delivered fast — without agency bloat.”

## Primary conversions
1) Buy 24-hr landing page ($499)
2) Buy Website-in-a-Week ($2,000)
3) Book a call (fallback CTA)

## Trust signals
- Portfolio screenshots
- Delivery timeline
- Clear scope rules
- Guarantee (limited)
- Reviews/testimonials
