# Daily Morning Checklist (30 Minutes)

## Purpose
Run this every weekday morning to keep lead flow, follow-up speed, and revenue execution on track.

## Time Block (30 minutes total)

### 0) Setup (2 min)
- [ ] Open:
  - `docs/15-WEEKLY-GROWTH-DASHBOARD.md`
  - `docs/templates/lead-log-template.csv` (or your live lead log sheet)
  - `docs/templates/outreach-tracker-template.csv` (or your live tracker)
  - Email + calendar + contact form inbox

### 1) Scoreboard Pulse (5 min)
- [ ] Check yesterday:
  - Leads in
  - Calls booked
  - Proposals sent
  - Closes
  - New MRR
- [ ] Mark today’s status:
  - `P0` revenue blocker
  - `P1` growth move
  - `P2` optimization move

### 2) Pipeline Moves (10 min)
- [ ] Follow up every lead with no response older than 24 hours.
- [ ] Send at least 3 follow-up touches (email or text).
- [ ] Move stalled leads to a clear next step:
  - Book call
  - Send proposal
  - Close-lost / nurture
- [ ] Confirm today’s calls and prepare quick notes for each.

### 3) Outreach Block (10 min)
- [ ] Send 10 new personalized outreach messages.
- [ ] Log each touch in outreach tracker.
- [ ] Send 1 partner outreach message (agency, photographer, IT, ads manager).
- [ ] Use templates from `docs/16-OUTREACH-TEMPLATES.md`.

### 4) Proof + Distribution (3 min)
- [ ] Publish or schedule 1 proof-based post:
  - case study snippet
  - conversion win
  - audit insight
- [ ] Add one CTA in the post: `Free Website Analysis` or `Book a 10-Minute Call`.

## Daily Minimum Output
- 10 new outreach
- 3 follow-ups
- 1 proof post
- 1 clear `P0` task completed by end of day

## Red Flags (Same-Day Fix Required)
- Zero follow-ups by noon
- No proposals sent for 3+ days
- No new outreach for 2+ days
- Calls booked but no close plan prepared
- Inbox replies older than 12 business hours
